Sports Massage

What is Sports Massage?

Sports massage is specifically designed for active individuals and athletes. It combines various techniques to enhance performance, prevent injuries, and aid in recovery.

Benefits

  • Prevents and treats injuries
  • Improves flexibility and range of motion
  • Reduces muscle soreness and fatigue
  • Enhances athletic performance
  • Speeds up recovery time
  • Reduces muscle tension and stress

What to Expect

Your sports massage session includes:

  • Assessment of problem areas
  • Targeted deep tissue work
  • Stretching and range of motion exercises
  • Compression and friction techniques
  • Post-treatment recommendations

Types of Treatment

  • Pre-event massage - Prepare muscles for activity
  • Post-event massage - Aid recovery after competition
  • Maintenance massage - Regular upkeep between events
  • Rehabilitation massage - Help with injury recovery

Potential Risks

Consider these factors:

  • Temporary muscle soreness
  • Not recommended immediately after acute injury
  • May need medical clearance for certain conditions
  • Should be avoided with certain injuries or conditions
