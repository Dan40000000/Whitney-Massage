Swedish Massage

What is Swedish Massage?

Swedish massage is a gentle, relaxing form of massage that uses long strokes, kneading, and circular movements to ease muscle tension and promote overall wellness.

Benefits

  • Reduces muscle tension and stiffness
  • Improves circulation and blood flow
  • Promotes relaxation and stress relief
  • Helps with flexibility and range of motion
  • Can improve sleep quality
  • Reduces anxiety and depression symptoms

What to Expect

During your session, you'll lie on a massage table under a sheet. The therapist will use a combination of:

  • Long, gliding strokes (effleurage)
  • Kneading movements (petrissage)
  • Circular motions
  • Tapping or percussion
  • Vibration techniques

Potential Risks

Swedish massage is generally very safe, but some considerations include:

  • Temporary soreness after treatment
  • Not recommended for certain medical conditions (consult your doctor)
  • May not be suitable during pregnancy without modification
