Deep Tissue Massage

What is Deep Tissue Massage?

Deep tissue massage targets the deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue using firm pressure and slow strokes. It's particularly effective for chronic muscle tension and injuries.

Benefits

  • Relieves chronic muscle tension and knots
  • Helps with muscle injuries and strains
  • Reduces inflammation and pain
  • Improves posture
  • Breaks up scar tissue
  • Increases range of motion

What to Expect

During a deep tissue massage, you can expect:

  • Firm pressure on problem areas
  • Focus on specific muscle groups
  • Work on adhesions and knots
  • Slower, more intense strokes
  • Possible mild discomfort during treatment

Potential Risks

While generally safe, deep tissue massage has some considerations:

  • Soreness for 1-2 days after treatment
  • Not recommended for certain health conditions
  • May aggravate recent injuries
  • Not suitable during pregnancy
  • Should be avoided if you have blood clots
