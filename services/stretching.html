Deep Stretching Work

What is Deep Stretching Work?

Deep stretching work combines assisted stretching with therapeutic techniques to improve flexibility, release tension, and enhance range of motion. This specialized treatment helps lengthen muscles and fascia while promoting better body alignment.

Benefits

  • Increases flexibility and range of motion
  • Improves posture and alignment
  • Reduces muscle tension and stiffness
  • Enhances athletic performance
  • Prevents injuries
  • Promotes faster recovery
  • Reduces chronic pain

What to Expect

During your deep stretching session:

  • Initial assessment of tight areas and limitations
  • Gentle warm-up to prepare muscles
  • Assisted stretching techniques
  • PNF (Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation) stretching
  • Joint mobilization
  • Myofascial release techniques

Target Areas

  • Hip flexors and hamstrings
  • Lower back and spine
  • Shoulders and neck
  • Chest and upper back
  • Calves and ankles
  • IT band and quads

Potential Risks

While generally safe, consider these factors:

  • Temporary muscle soreness
  • Not recommended for acute injuries
  • May need modification for certain conditions
  • Should be gentle if you have hypermobility
  • Requires communication about comfort levels
