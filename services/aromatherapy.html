Aromatherapy Massage

What is Aromatherapy Massage?

Aromatherapy massage combines the therapeutic benefits of massage with essential oils to enhance physical and emotional well-being through your sense of smell.

Benefits

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves sleep quality
  • Boosts mood and mental clarity
  • Relieves muscle tension
  • Supports immune system function
  • Enhances relaxation response

What to Expect

Your aromatherapy massage experience includes:

  • Consultation to select appropriate essential oils
  • Gentle to moderate pressure massage
  • Custom blend of essential oils
  • Inhalation and topical application of oils
  • Relaxing atmosphere

Essential Oils Used

  • Lavender - Relaxation and sleep
  • Peppermint - Energy and focus
  • Eucalyptus - Respiratory support
  • Chamomile - Calming and soothing
  • Tea Tree - Immune support

Potential Risks

Consider these factors before booking:

  • Possible skin sensitivity to certain oils
  • Not recommended during first trimester of pregnancy
  • May interact with certain medications
  • Tell your therapist about any allergies
